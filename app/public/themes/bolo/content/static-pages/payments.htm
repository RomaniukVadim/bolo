DEPOSIT FAQS


Q: How long does a deposit take to be credited into my account?

A: Deposits are generally credited into your account immediately. Once a deposit is confirmed, you will see the amount in your balance on the BOLO BET site - displayed at the top, next to your username. 

 

Q: I just made a deposit, but I do not see it in my BOLO BET balance...

A: Once a deposit is completed successfully, you will see this in your BOLO BET balance, displayed at the top, next to your username. 

We will  follow your instructions and transfer your funds to the requested account(s). You will then see the funds in that specified account(s). During operating hours and upon your deposit being successful, we aim to complete requests within 15 minutes. 

Please note - when funds are moved into your requested account(s) - your balance on the BOLO BET website will decrease.

For example, you have zero funds in your BOLO BET account. You deposit 1,000.00 and instruct us to transfer this to your Orbit Exchange account. Your balance on BOLO BET will show 1,000.00 and then 0.00 once funds are transferred to Orbit. 

 

Q: What is the minimum deposit amount?

A: The minimum deposit amount is 100CNY per transaction for all payment methods.


Q: What is the maximum deposit amount?

A: Each payment method has a maximum deposit amount, you can view the min. and max. deposit amounts above. You can make more than one deposit with each payment method. If you wish to deposit more than the maximum, you can just make multiple deposits. 


Q: Is there an admin fee when I make a deposit?

A: There are no administration fees for making a deposit.


Q: Can I ask my friend to make a deposit for me with their account?

A: In order to meet the International AML standards, deposits can only be made from the account holder’s account.

 

Q: How do I make a deposit via  ?

A: To make a deposit from your mobile phone:

  1. Go to our Payments page and select the Deposit button for  .

  2. Enter in the Deposit Amount, Currency and Message. Click “Continue” and you’ll be redirected to a page with a QR code.

  3. Use another mobile phone / tablet to scan the QR code via your  App.

  4. A pop up will appear with the payment details, follow the instructions to complete your deposit.

  5. If the deposit is successful, a confirmation message will be sent to your chat section and you will see a “thank you” message on the BOLO BET screen from the device that was displaying the QR code.  


To make a deposit from your desktop:

  1. Go to our Payments page and select the Deposit button for .

  2. Enter in the Deposit Amount, Currency and Message. Click “Continue” and you’ll be redirected to a page with a QR code.

  3. Use your mobile phone to scan the QR code via your App.

  4. A pop up will appear with the payment details, follow the instructions to complete your deposit.

  5. If the deposit is successful, a confirmation message will be sent to your chat section and you will see a “thank you” message on the BOLO BET site on your desktop. 

 

Q: I cannot get the QR code working on my mobile....

A: At this time, long pressing the QR code or screen-grabbing the QR code, saving it to your photo album, then opening it in the App has been disabled by . You will need a second device to scan the QR code to make a deposit from your mobile. We aim to offer more methods to deposit via as soon as they become available. 


Q: How do I make a deposit via?

A: Go to our Payments page and select the Deposit button for Desktop or Mobile. Enter in the Deposit Amount, Currency and Message. Click “Continue” and you’ll be redirected to theplatform page - please remember to have your mobile phone or token at hand. Enter your details to complete the deposit. Once confirmed, click on the “Go back to Merchant” button to return to the BOLO BET website. 


Q: Do I always have to enter my card number onmobile?

A: You will need to enter your card number the first time you make a deposit on each new mobile phone. Select the “Remember your card number” option, next time you’ll only need to enter your Pin and SMS Verification code when depositing from the same mobile phone.


Q: How do I view my deposits?

A: Once logged in, you will have access to a Statement of your accounts. Click on “Statements” from the top navigation. Here you will be able to view all of your deposits and withdrawals.
