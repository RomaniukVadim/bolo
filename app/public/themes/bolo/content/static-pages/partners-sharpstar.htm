ABOUT SHARPSTAR

SharpStar is the first sports spread betting operator to launch in Asia. The idea behind their product was to combine the excitement of buying and selling options on financial markets and passion for sports.  

Potential winnings and the sheer variety of markets are greater in spread betting than on fixed odds - the more you know, the more you can win. By using your sports knowledge, they claim that you can boost your profits in comparison to betting on fixed odds. In fixed odds betting, you are betting on particular outcome - it is a simple win, lose or push. Whereas in sports spread betting, it’s not fixed and is very fluid throughout an event. 

There’s always a higher and lower figure, that’s what is called, ‘the spread’. SharpStar make predictions, then it’s up to you to decide if the final outcome will be higher or lower, more or less, faster or slower. If you think their price is too low, you would BUY (higher figure). If you think their quote is too high, you would SELL (lower figure).

Sports spread betting relies more than just the outcome, but your payout is dependant on how right you are or how wrong you are - and your stake size. It’s a very exciting opportunity to boost your profits if you have good sports knowledge.
