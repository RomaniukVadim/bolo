How to Make a Withdrawal


All withdrawals are made via Bank Transfer. Please send your withdrawal requests to cs@bolo888.com including the information below:

  • Withdrawal Amount

  • From Which Account

  • Full Name of Cardholder

  • Bank Account Number

  • Bank Name

  • Branch Address (Province/City)

Once your request is received we will notify you via email to let you know your request is being processed. Please note: Withdrawal requests are processed during our operating hours only.


WITHDRAWAL FAQS


Q: How long does a withdrawal take to be credited into my account?

A: All withdrawals will be processed via Bank Transfer, please allow up to 1-2 working days for the funds to reach your account. Amounts will be deducted from your betting account, moved to your BOLO BET wallet and taken out of your BOLO BET wallet within 15 minutes during our operating hours.  Payments can be issued on the same day if the withdrawal request is received by 1800BST/GMT and will clear into your account within 1 to 2 working days max. Please note: Banks may not process payments between 11pm-9am (GMT+8).


Q: What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

A: The minimum withdrawal amount is 100 CNY per transaction.


Q: What is the maximum withdrawal amount?

A: The maximum withdrawal amount is based on the funds available to withdrawal from your betting account(s). 


Q: Is there an administration fee for withdrawals?

A: There are no administration fees for making a withdrawal.


Q: What information do I need to provide to make my first withdrawal?

A: We will need to confirm your identity. Upon making your first withdrawal, our customer support team will ask you for your Identification Card number. For withdrawals greater than 10,000 CNY, we will need a copy (back and front) of your Identification Card. Please ensure the name on your ID card and Bank Account name match. 


Q: Can I make a withdrawal to my friend’s account?

A: In order to meet International AML standards, withdrawals can only be made to the account holder’s accounts.


Q: How do I view my withdrawals?

A: Once logged in, you will have access to a Statement of your accounts. Click on “Statements” from the top navigation. Here you will be able to view all of your deposits and withdrawals.
