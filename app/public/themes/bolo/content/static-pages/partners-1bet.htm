1Bet are now a leading online sportsbook. They are very similar to IBC (Maxbet) in their look and feel and also settle bets at a partial amount of 80%.

 

They have a wide variety of betting opportunities across a range of sports – specialising in Asian Handicaps. Like many other Asian Sportsbooks they welcome arbitrage business, do note the 1×2 prices are not as competitive. You can find better elsewhere.

 

1Bet are owned by Bellona N.V. with offices located in Curacao. Gaming activity is operated and regulated under license no. 8048/JAZ issued by the Gaming Commission of Curacao.

 

We have noticed that 1Bet offer very competitive odds, have early lines available across their football product and a good in-play offering with settlement at 80%. BOLO BET have not been informed of any voided bets.
