M8bet has fast become one of Asia’s leading online sportsbooks. They’ve been regulated and licensed by First Cagayan (Philippines) since May 2012.

 

M8bet offers more than 3,000 in-play sporting events every week, focusing on soccer, basketball and tennis. Their football product is by far their best and offers all the major football leagues down to regional football events, all at very competitive prices and good levels of liquidity available both pre-match and in-play.

 

Once signed in, the look and feel of the sportsbook is of vast similarity to IBC (Maxbet) therefore very easy to navigate and will feel familiar to most players. M8 do not VOID bets, but they do not like Arbitrage players therefore please avoid this type of business when using them as you may experience a VOIDED bet.
