ABOUT

Founded in the United Kingdom, the birthplace of modern football and the hub for online sports betting, BOLO BET is an exclusive sports betting service that provides fast and secured access to a network of bookmakers, spread betting and exchange betting accounts – all through a single wallet and registration.

The BOLO BET team has over 20 years of experience in the sports betting industry. We are a truly global team with members coming from around the world. Combined, our team speaks more than 5 languages. Our expertise lies in brokerage, trading, pro-punting, management, finance, corporate structures and customer service. We understand what our clients need and which bookmakers or trading platforms are best suited to their requirements.

Our Aim: To provide our clients with a seamless betting experience by using our single wallet system. Our team is highly trained to provide professional and discreet customer support.


Why BOLO BET?

1) We offer a single wallet, one-stop secured betting solution.

2) Bespoke service, we assess your requirements and offer you the best solution.

3) Our trusted partners provide the best prices across thousands of markets.
